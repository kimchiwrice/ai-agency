Our Story

Started With One Rescue.
Now We Serve Thousands.

Paws & Co started in 2018 when our founder rescued a scruffy mutt named Biscuit from the Sacramento SPCA. That experience revealed how hard it was to find quality pet care, honest advice, and a shop that actually cared.

So we built one. A neighborhood spot where every pet gets treated like our own, where the staff knows your dog's name, and where you can trust every product on the shelf.
